Scramble Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for delicious rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

Method
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  1. Melt butter in frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has melted mix add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you're making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
